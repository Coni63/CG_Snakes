  Rules

The game is played on a board of height rows by width columns. This board contains numSnakes snakes and apples. Each snakes likes only one apple. The initial length of a snake is 3

Every turn, the snake can move in one of adjacents tiles using a given action U, D, R or L.
Every snake has an initial amount of energy (= height * width). Every action consume 1 energy. When the snake eats an apple, the energy is reset. In addition, the snake's length increase by 1.



The game ends when:
  • One snake bites itself or another snake
  • You provide an invalid output (not case sensitive)
  • One snake runs out of energy
  • There is no space left
